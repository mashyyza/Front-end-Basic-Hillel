About BO "Dim Sirka"

House of Sulfur is a publicly accessible group that unites volunteers of BO "House of Sulfur", animal owners, caring citizens who share our idea of the value of every life.
🟩Our mission is to rescue homeless dogs and cats that are in trouble (car injury, illness, abuse, etc.), find a temporary shelter for animals and adopt them.
🟥Our philosophy is that every life is important.
🟨How can we help?
1. Car assistance 🚗 - transportation of animals to the clinic, for a stay, to a new home, etc.
2. Overstay 🏠 - temporary placement of an animal during its treatment, socialization, and search for a home.
3. Material assistance 🐶🐱 - food (dry and wet food), diapers, unnecessary things, dishes, blankets, etc.
4. Financial assistance 💰 - targeted assistance (the name of the animal is indicated) or targeted assistance (the item of expenses is indicated - "treatment", "vaccines", "food", etc.) is carried out on the key card of the Head of the NGO "House of Sulfur" Kohut N.V .
